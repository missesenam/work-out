the best nutrients and healthy snacks for you

eau

It's critical to remain hydrated whether you're a serious athlete or just like exercising for fun. Hydration refers to drinking enough water before, during, and after activity. Water keeps your joints lubricated and your body temperature stable. It aids in the transportation of nutrients, which provides energy and keeps you healthy. Your body cannot function optimally if you are dehydrated. You may have fatigue, muscular cramps, dizziness, or other significant symptoms.

ways to consume

  • warm water
  • at room temperature

fenugrec

Fenugreek is an annual leguminous plant native to India and North Africa. Yes, it is rather exotic! Fenugreek has been said to offer several health advantages and is widely utilized in traditional medicine.
Fenugreek has traditionally been used for therapeutic reasons in the form of a powder derived from the seeds. Fenugreek can also be used in the form of a paste, which is applied to the skin and utilized as an anti-inflammatory. However, Fenugreek has been related to increased strength and body composition, as well as impacts on insulin control and hyperglycemia in diabetic individuals. more about fenugrec

ways to consume

  • Fenugreek Tea, check how to make fenugrec tea
  • A teaspoon of fenugreek powder mixed in a warm cup of water
  • A teaspoon of fenugreek powder mixed in a warm cup of milk
  • Use the fenugrec in food as spice

Fruit And Greek Yogurt

This is a fantastic combination. The fruit is high in carbs, while Greek yogurt is high in protein. Greek yogurt provides nearly double the protein, less carbohydrates, and half the salt of normal yogurt. What ties them together? The carbohydrates in the fruit are immediately broken down and used as fuel throughout your workout, whilst the protein is kept for a bit longer and is used to prevent muscle damage, making it a wonderful complement.

milk

Your muscles require carbs and protein to rebuild after an exercise, and the sooner you can feed your muscles, the faster they will recover. Milk gives your muscles with the carbs and protein they require in a highly handy and hydrated form that is great for taking soon after exercise.

smoothie

Exercise is only a component of your total health journey. It is just as essential what you put into your body as what you receive out of it. This entails refilling your body with protein and amino acids to aid in muscle repair and rebuilding. After each workout, the easiest way to accomplish this is with a protein smoothie. A smoothie contains all of the nutrients required to grow and repair muscle tissue. A high-protein diet keeps you feeling fuller for longer. Protein aids in the growth of lean muscle and toning. A smoothie aids recovery, so you won't be sore the next day! more about smoothie
