PRIVACY POLICY

We respect your privacy here at ayofitness. This privacy statement outlines how ayofitness may gather, use, and share information about you via its website or app. We recommend that you review this policy periodically to understand how we handle your personal information because it may change as we update or develop our services. You consent to ayofitness using information about you in line with this privacy statement if you use this website and its services.

  1. What information do we collect?

    2. Currently, when users choose to fill out an e-mail submission form to ask questions about our business and about membership, Ayofitness collects information from them solely for contact purposes (such as name, e-mail address, business and/or home address, age, zip code, and travel preferences). Other optional information, such as extra contact information and specific demographic data (such as a phone number and gender), is occasionally requested.

  2. How do we use your information?

    3. Your information may be used by us for a variety of things, such as providing and maintaining your Ayofitness membership, offering member support, maintaining the website and mobile application, informing you of special deals, promotions, and company news via email and/or text message, and compiling usage statistics and other information about how you use the website's services.

  3. How do we use your email information?

    4. We at Ayofitness appreciate your privacy concerns. To deliver a more individualized and pertinent experience, we gather email information. When you sign up, inquire about membership, or ask for a free trial guest pass, we will ask for your email address so that we can send you emails about special deals, Ayofitness news, and future club openings. Providing updated information will serve as a "opt back-in" if you previously chose not to receive communications from us. Simply click the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any of our emails to choose not to receive promotional emails from us. Although we make an effort to refresh our email list periodically, you might hear from someone else before we are able to unsubscribe you.Please be aware that you will still get communications from Member Services about your member account. Any of your available contact methods (including email, mailing, phone, and fax) may be used by Ayofitness to get in touch with you for routine administration tasks including freeze requests, online purchases, and significant service notifications.

  4. How do we use your phone number?

    5. Your consent to receive SMS text alerts from Ayofitness is obtained when you offer your mobile phone number, and you also attest that you are the regular user of the provided phone number. You can use an automatic calling system to send text notifications. The purchase of any products or services does not require your approval, which you can withdraw at any time by sending the message STOP in response to any SMS text message you receive. Data and message rates can be charged.

  5. How we share your information:

    6. Your information may be distributed in the following ways: With independent contractors: To carry out tasks on our behalf, we hire other businesses and people. In order to fulfill your order, charge your credit card (or process your check or money order, as appropriate), fill your order, enhance the functionality of our site, perform statistical and data analyses, deliver your order and send you promotional emails from us, remove repetitive information from customer lists, provide marketing assistance and targeted advertising, and provide you with promotional emails from us, we may disclose your information to third parties for any of the aforementioned reasons. For instance, in order to authenticate your payment, we must give your credit card number, and in order for the delivery service to deliver the things you ordered, we must disclose your mailing address. They have access to the personal data required to carry out their duties, but they are not permitted to use it for other purposes.

  6. Do we use cookies?

    7. To improve your experience on our website, we use cookies. For instance, a cookie helps us to remember the region or particular club page you have seen so you won't have to choose it again when you visit our site in the future. Your web browser (such as Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Internet Explorer) can be configured to either alert you each time a cookie is sent or to disable all cookies. For these instructions, refer to the Help menu on your browser. You won't have access to some of the features that improve your user experience on our site if you disable your cookies.

  7. How can we keep kids safe on our website?

    8. On our website, we're dedicated to creating a secure environment for kids. Users are notified they must be at least 18 years old or have parental agreement to give personally identifying information to us when we gather information for membership inquiry purposes. To keep kids safe when using Crunch.com and other parts of the Internet, we advise parents to keep an eye on their children's online activities.

  8. Does this Privacy Policy apply to external links found on this Web site?

    9. Please be aware that even though this website might contain links to other websites, when you click on one of those links, you will leave Ayofitness.com and go to the other website. Because these websites' privacy policies can differ from ours, we recommend you to read theirs as well. We disclaim any liability for the content or privacy policies of such websites.

    10. For other inquiries about our clubs, please get in touch with us here.
